What could the Horizon Forbidden West story be about?

Gamers play as Aloy, a member of one of many tribes that stumbles upon the origins of the mechanical beasts the rule the world. They were created by a company named Faro Automated Solutions, nearly 1,000 ago as "peacemakers", but need biomass for fuel. Eventually, they consumed so much of the Earth's biosphere that it eradicated most humans. But there was a plan B.
